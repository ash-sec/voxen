## H2: What to Look for When You Hire a LinkedIn Ghostwriter

Here's where people go wrong. They search for the cheapest option, or they hire someone who writes well but has never spent serious time on LinkedIn specifically.

LinkedIn content Australia has its own rhythm. What works on Twitter doesn't land the same way here. What performs in the US doesn't always translate to an Australian professional audience. The platform has nuance and you want someone who actually understands it.

A few things worth checking before you commit:

**They ask a lot of questions.** If a ghostwriter takes your money without a proper onboarding process, no voice notes, no intake form, no call, that's a red flag. They cannot write in your voice if they don't know your voice.

**They have examples that don't look copy-pasted.** Ask to see posts they've written across different clients. If they all sound the same, that's your answer.

**They understand your industry.** Whether you're in finance, property, trades or professional services, the ghostwriter should at least be able to hold a conversation about your world. They don't need to be an expert. But they can't be completely clueless either.

**They post for the platform, not their portfolio.** Some writers are technically good but they're optimising for what looks impressive in a case study rather than what actually performs on LinkedIn.

## H2: LinkedIn Ghostwriting Service Australia: Red Flags to Watch Out For

A few things that should give you pause.

**Guaranteed follower numbers.** Nobody can ethically promise you X followers in Y days. LinkedIn growth Australia comes from consistency, quality and relevance over time. Anyone promising fast results is selling you something you won't be happy with.

**No revision process.** You should always have input on the posts before they go live, at least in the early stages while the ghostwriter is learning your voice.

**One size fits all content.** If you're a mortgage broker in Brisbane and your ghostwriter is sending you posts that could have been written for anyone in any country in any industry, that's a problem. LinkedIn for mortgage brokers, financial advisers, real estate agents and business owners each requires a different approach.

**No real onboarding.** A LinkedIn ghostwriter Australia worth hiring will spend proper time understanding you before they write a single word.
