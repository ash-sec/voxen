## H2: How to Know If a LinkedIn Ghostwriting Service Australia Is Worth the Price

Good questions to ask any service before you commit:

**Do they do an onboarding call?** If a service just sends you a questionnaire and gets cracking, be cautious. Your voice is nuanced. A quick form won't capture it.

**Can they share examples in your industry?** Not generic samples. Actual examples from professionals similar to you. LinkedIn for business owners Australia looks different from LinkedIn for financial advisers Australia or LinkedIn for real estate agents, so industry experience matters.

**What does revision look like?** One round? Unlimited? Turnaround time?

**Do they offer profile optimisation?** Some services bundle this in. It's worth asking because a great post bringing people to a mediocre profile is a waste.

**Are they writing in your voice or their voice?** This is the big one. If the content could belong to anyone, it's not doing its job.
