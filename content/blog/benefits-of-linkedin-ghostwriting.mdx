## Is LinkedIn Ghostwriting Actually Ethical?

Worth addressing, because people do ask.

Ghostwriting has existed for centuries. Political speeches, memoirs, business books, newspaper columns. The ideas belong to the person whose name is on it. The craft of putting those ideas into readable, shareable form belongs to the writer. That's a legitimate collaboration, not a deception.

On LinkedIn specifically, nobody is pretending you personally typed every word. What people care about is whether your ideas are real, your insights are genuine, and your perspective is authentic. Ghostwriting, when done well, preserves all of that.

What it removes is the friction between having something worth saying and actually saying it.
