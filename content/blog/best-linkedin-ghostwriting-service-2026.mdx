## H2: The Red Flags to Watch For When You're Comparing LinkedIn Ghostwriting Services Australia

Look, there are a lot of providers out there promising results. Some are great. Some will hand you templated content with your name swapped in at the top.

Here's what to watch for:

**Generic onboarding.** If a service doesn't ask about your tone, your audience, your opinions on your industry, that's a problem. Good LinkedIn content creation Australia starts with understanding who you actually are.

**No revision process.** Your voice isn't static. It takes a few rounds to get right. Any decent LinkedIn ghostwriter Australia worth their rate will expect some back and forth early on.

**Vague deliverables.** "We write LinkedIn content" is not a service offering. You want to know exactly how many posts, how often, what format, and what the turnaround looks like.

**Portfolio that all sounds the same.** If every sample reads in the same voice, that's a ghostwriter who writes in their voice, not yours.
