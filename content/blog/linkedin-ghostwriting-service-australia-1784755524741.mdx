## How to Choose the Right LinkedIn Ghostwriter Australia

A few things worth thinking through before you commit to anyone.

Do they actually interview you? Or do they churn out generic content based on your job title and industry? The difference in output is enormous. Your LinkedIn posts should sound like you, not like a press release.

Do they understand the platform? **LinkedIn growth Australia** isn't achieved by just writing well. It requires knowing what formats are working right now, how the algorithm treats different post structures, and what your specific audience actually wants to read.

Do they have a revision process? Your first few posts together will need adjusting. Any honest writer will tell you it takes a few weeks to genuinely nail someone's voice.

And practically speaking: what does the turnaround look like? If you have to chase them for content every week, that defeats the purpose of outsourcing in the first place.
