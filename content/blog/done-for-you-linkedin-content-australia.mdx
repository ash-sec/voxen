## How to Know If Outsourcing Your LinkedIn Content Is Right for You

Ask yourself three questions.

Do you actually want to write your own LinkedIn posts, or do you just feel like you should? There's a difference. If you genuinely enjoy it, maybe carve out the time. If it's a chore you've been avoiding for months, that's your answer.

Do you have a clear point of view on your industry? You need this regardless of whether you write your own content or work with someone else. The ghostwriter can craft the words. They can't invent your thinking for you. If you've got opinions, stories and experience, a ghostwriter can work wonders.

Is LinkedIn actually a channel that could move the needle for your business? For most B2B professionals in Australia, yes. Full stop. If your clients are other businesses, if referrals drive your pipeline, if you're trying to build a reputation in your industry, LinkedIn is where it happens.

If you answered yes to those three, done for you LinkedIn content is probably worth exploring.
