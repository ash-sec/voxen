## What "Done For You LinkedIn Content" Actually Involves

People assume outsourcing means handing over a login and getting random posts back. Good done for you LinkedIn content doesn't work like that.

The process usually starts with a proper voice capture. A LinkedIn ghostwriter will interview you, go through your past content, understand your opinions and the way you talk, then build posts that sound like you wrote them at your best, not like you wrote them at 10pm after a long day.

Here's what a proper setup typically covers:

- **Weekly or fortnightly drafts** delivered for your review before anything goes live

The goal isn't to replace your thinking. It's to capture your thinking and put it in front of the right people, consistently.

## What to Look For When You Hire a LinkedIn Ghostwriter

Not all services are built the same. When you're looking to hire a LinkedIn ghostwriter or explore LinkedIn ghostwriting service Australia options, there are a few things worth checking.

**What does ongoing communication look like?** You'll want a process that keeps you involved without consuming your time. Monthly check-ins, easy revision requests, that kind of thing.
