## What to Look For in a LinkedIn Ghostwriting Service Australia

Not all services are built the same. A few things worth checking before you commit.

**They should interview you properly.** If a ghostwriter doesn't want to understand your background, your clients, and your point of view before they start writing, the content will be generic. Generic LinkedIn content is basically invisible.

**They should show you samples in different voices.** One person's posts should not sound identical to another's. If they do, the ghostwriter is templating, not actually capturing voice.

**They should understand your industry.** This matters more than it sounds. A ghostwriter who writes for tradies, mortgage brokers, and financial advisers needs to understand what those clients actually care about, not just the surface-level jargon.

**Turnaround and communication.** You need to know when drafts land in your inbox, how many revisions you get, and who you contact if something needs a tweak. Ambiguity here causes frustration fast.
