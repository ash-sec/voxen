## How to Tell If LinkedIn Ghostwriting Is Right for You

Honestly, not everyone needs a ghostwriter. Some coaches genuinely love writing and just need a bit of structure.

But if any of these sound familiar, it's probably worth considering:

**You've got ideas but they never become posts.** The gap between thinking something and writing it publicly feels massive. Weeks go by.

**Your posting is inconsistent.** You post five times in a burst of motivation then disappear for a month. Algorithms don't love this. Neither do potential clients.

**You're spending hours on a single post.** That's not sustainable. Your time is worth more than that.

**You're not sure what to write about.** You know your stuff, but translating it into LinkedIn content that resonates with your ideal client feels murky.

If two or more of those landed, LinkedIn ghostwriting for coaches is probably a smart move.
