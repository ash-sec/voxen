## What Does a LinkedIn Ghostwriter in Australia Actually Do?

The job varies depending on who you hire, but broadly speaking, a LinkedIn ghostwriter takes what's in your head and turns it into content that sounds like you wrote it on a good day.

That usually means weekly posts, sometimes daily ones, written to reflect your tone, your opinions, your industry. Some ghostwriters also handle strategy, formatting, hashtag research, even engagement advice.

What they're not doing is making stuff up. Good ghostwriters interview you, ask questions, pull out your stories and your perspective. The ideas stay yours. The writing just gets done properly.
