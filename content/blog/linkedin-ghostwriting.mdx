## What LinkedIn Ghostwriting Actually Involves

Most people picture ghostwriting as someone typing out your thoughts while you dictate from a leather chair. It's not quite that dramatic.

A good LinkedIn ghostwriter Australia-based or otherwise will start by getting to know how you think. They'll ask about your opinions, your client stories, the stuff that keeps you up at night professionally. Then they take that raw material and shape it into posts people actually want to read.

The process usually looks something like this: a brief intake call or questionnaire, a batch of draft posts, your feedback, revisions, then a steady stream of content that sounds genuinely like you. Not a corporate robot. You.
