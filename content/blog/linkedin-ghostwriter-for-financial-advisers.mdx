## How to Actually Choose a LinkedIn Ghostwriter for Financial Advisers

Not all ghostwriters are equal. Honestly, quite a few aren't even close.

When you're evaluating someone to handle your **LinkedIn content Australia**, ask them these things:

**Do they understand financial services compliance?** If they hesitate or can't give you a concrete answer, that's your answer.

**Can they show you samples written for other finance professionals?** Generic samples from other industries won't tell you much.

**What's their process for capturing your voice?** Good ghostwriters interview you, observe how you talk, read your existing content. They don't just ask you to fill out a form.

**How do they handle revisions?** Your name is going on this content. You need to feel comfortable with it.

**What does consistency actually look like?** Posting twice in a week and then going quiet for a month does more harm than not posting at all. Ask what their delivery schedule looks like.

**LinkedIn growth Australia** for financial advisers is genuinely achievable. But it requires consistency over months, not a burst of activity followed by silence.
