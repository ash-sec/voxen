## H2: What Kind of LinkedIn Content Works for Mortgage Brokers

Not everything performs equally. Here's what actually cuts through for brokers specifically.

### Client Stories (Anonymised, Obviously)

"Helped a first home buyer get approved after two bank knockbacks" performs way better than "I help clients achieve their property goals." Specificity is everything. People want proof you can handle tricky situations.

### Rate Commentary and Market Takes

You don't need to be an economist. Just share your genuine read on what's happening with rates, what clients are asking you this week, how you're navigating it. That kind of content builds credibility fast.

### Behind the Scenes

What does your day actually look like? What surprised you about a recent deal? What do most people get wrong about borrowing capacity? These posts feel real because they are real, and they perform accordingly.

### Addressing the Fear

Buying property is genuinely stressful for most Australians. Posts that acknowledge the anxiety, the confusion, the "am I doing the right thing" feeling, those resonate deeply. It's not soft content. It's smart content.
