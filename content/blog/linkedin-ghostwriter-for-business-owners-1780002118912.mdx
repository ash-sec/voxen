Do they have examples of LinkedIn-specific work? Blog writing and LinkedIn writing are different disciplines. The format, the tone, the structure. All different.

Do they offer revisions? Your voice will take a few rounds to nail. Any decent ghostwriter knows that and builds it into the process.
