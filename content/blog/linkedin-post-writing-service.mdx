## H2: How to Choose a LinkedIn Ghostwriting Service in Australia That's Actually Good

Not all services are equal. Obvious statement, but worth unpacking.

The cheap options tend to be generic. Templates with your name swapped in. Posts that could belong to anyone. That kind of content doesn't build a personal brand, it just fills a posting schedule with noise.

When you're evaluating a **LinkedIn ghostwriting Australia** service, ask these questions:

**Can they show you real examples from real clients?** Not just testimonials. Actual posts that performed.

**What does the revision process look like?** You should always have final say before anything goes live. Always.
